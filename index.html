The Pomodoro Technique is a time-management and productivity technique invented by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It uses intervals of 25 minutes of work and 5 minutes of break time to maximize efficient work to break ratio. I've integrated this into a rich text editor in order to encourage active and effective note taking, as well as improve the life of writers and students. Thanks for visiting!
