PomodoroText Manual

    PomodoroText is a time-saving and productivity based writing software, as mentioned several times in previous pages. There are many features, such as bold (denoted by a bold B), italic (denoted by an italic I), underline (denoted by an underlined U), and more. As well, saving in TXT Format is supported, and a Pomodoro inspired timing system - the focus of this manual.


    Until a button (seen as play or pause) is pressed, the bar denoting elapsed time will remain blue. Upon pressing the "play" button, work mode is activated, setting a 25 minute timer and counting down second by second. Upon pressing the "pause" button, break mode is activated, setting a 5 minute timer instead. You are intended to alternate these modes for around 4 "loops", until you get to the 4th break. Then, you may choose to either take a 20 minute break (resetting the timer each 5 minutes) or simply continue with your regular 5 minute break. This will ensure optimal ratio of work time to break time while preventing fatigue.


    Finally, we'll discuss saving. Since our save system works in BLOB with txt, rich text is not supported saving-wise. You can save by pressing the export-esque (top-right) button next to the others, and rename the file whatever you'd like before downloading. Happy writing!
