The Pomodoro Technique is a time-management and productivity technique invented by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It uses alternating intervals of 25 minutes of work and 5 minutes of break time to maximize and optimize the work to break ratio. I've integrated this into a rich text editor in order to encourage active and effective note taking, as well as improve the life of writers and students. Thanks for visiting! (Note - this project was created almost within 2-3 days - if you wanted to do something like this, you easily could!)
